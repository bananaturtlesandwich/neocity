9/9/24 my first website

this is where i write more in depth about myself i guess :p

i think because i've made such nice artwork for the other pages i'm a bit frozen on doing more with the website but i am getting unstuck. i think this kind of links to my overall perfectionism and the want to make everything to my standards

  • no long project will be perfect on the first pass
  • programs won't be optimised immediately
  • drawings won't look good if you don't work into them
  • you won't produce a good piece of writing without iteration

i don't know if this stems from anything and i've never really gone to a therapist to talk about this but my theory is i guess i was kind of a "gifted" kid? i've always done well academically in subjects i gave a fuck about and i received praise for it and doing better warranted even more (i think this also led to a bit of an inferiority complex though) plus my mum is an incredible human being and i wanted to achieve as high as she did

i mean i guess i kind of achieved that since i'm starting my first academic year at cambridge very soon but i suppose the point i'm putting across is i need to learn to let things be unfinished and a work in progress

i think i'll start by just typing out the shrine pages without even thinking about making them pretty and then i can decorate and stylise them to my heart's content later. i really want each shrine to embody its subject rather just be the same crappy spray of pixels you see everywhere else. and i suppose for the blog page i think an even trippier vibe would be cool since it's just a swirl of my thoughts but no ideas otherwise. i should probably sketch some :)

well this is my first blog post on my first website! ISN'T THAT COOL? i think i've always wanted a little outlet to just talk about stuff i love that no-one else really gives a shit about.

i initially heard about neocities from a great video about the indie web - i wholeheartedly agree that the mainstream internet has regressed and want to be a part of this lovely community. i've always been a big believer that the internet is for collaboration and sharing, especially in regards to open source software and learning resources. additionally, despite being born in the 2000s i missed the entirety of the early internet so i thought that this would be a nice way to try and experience it.

in terms of what i actually want this website to be i want it to be a place i can ramble about my interests, dump my thoughts and document my hobbies. one thing i really don't want to do is add to the pile of unoriginal trash that already exists here this should be a place which is completely, absolutely, truly mine

what will i actually do here then?

  • i want to create a ridiculous amount of shrines!
  • i want to talk about subjects on my mind or important to me!
  • i want to braindump intricacies of coding stuff!
  • i want to make and post covers of tracks i love!
  • i want to find other people who like things i like and be their friend!
  • i want to find incredible personal websites!

thank you neocities!
